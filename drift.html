The Problem

Rule-Based Tools Miss What Matters

CSPM tools like Prowler, Steampipe, and CloudQuery find misconfigurations. They cannot detect anomalous behavior — credential abuse, lateral movement, novel attack patterns. That requires ML.

The Solution

Drift — Open Core, Commercial Intelligence

An open-source connector collects CloudTrail and Prowler findings, normalizes to OCSF. The commercial layer adds ML anomaly detection, security graph, attack paths, and evidence automation.

How It Works

Connector runs in your AWS account. Data flows to the intelligence layer. You get anomalies, attack paths, and evidence — not just lists of findings.
